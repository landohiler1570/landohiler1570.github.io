The Vision

Range Data Services was founded on the principle that precision in data is the bedrock of investigative integrity.

Led by Lando Hiler, an MBA-qualified forensic analyst and PhD Candidate specializing in data anomaly research, the firm operates at the intersection of technical discovery and academic rigor. Our vision is to provide legal and corporate sectors with a standard of forensic analysis that goes beyond simple recovery—we identify the patterns that tell the story.

Based in El Reno, Oklahoma, we serve clients who require verifiable, executive-grade evidence in complex data environments. Whether it is digital discovery for legal proceedings or algorithmic anomaly detection for risk mitigation, our approach is surgical, objective, and exhaustive.
